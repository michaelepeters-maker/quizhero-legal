Delete Your QuizHero Account

Last updated April 13, 2026

You can request deletion of your QuizHero account and all associated data by following the steps below. Once deleted, your account cannot be recovered.

What gets deleted

  • Your account profile (username, email address)
  • Your game progress, XP, and level
  • Your Hero Credits (HC) balance
  • Your unlocked categories and heroes
  • Your friends list and social data
  • Your quiz history and scores

What is retained

  • Purchase records may be retained for up to 7 years for legal and accounting purposes. These records do not identify you by name.

How to request account deletion

Option 1 — Email us
Send an email to quizherosupport@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include the email address associated with your QuizHero account. We will process your request within 30 days.
Option 2 — In-app (coming soon)
A self-service account deletion option will be available directly within the QuizHero app settings.

If you have any questions, contact us at quizherosupport@gmail.com.
